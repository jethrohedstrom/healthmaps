Psychologist panel — A vs B

Two approachable redesigns of the bubble-map popover, side by side with the current one. Both lead with the real out-of-pocket cost and hide clinical detail behind More detail. Version A answers the questions people actually ask; Version B is a calm, glanceable stat card. Click “More detail” in each to feel the interaction.
